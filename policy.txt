Company Refund and Return Policy

Our company refund policy allows returns within 30 days of purchase.
Products must be unused and in their original packaging to qualify for a return.
To initiate a return, contact support@company.com with your order number.
Refunds are processed within 5 business days after we receive the returned item.
International orders may take up to 14 business days for refund processing.
We do not accept returns on digital products or downloadable software.
Sale items are final and cannot be returned or exchanged under any circumstances.

Shipping Policy

All orders are shipped within 2 business days of purchase confirmation.
Standard shipping takes 5 to 7 business days within the United States.
Express shipping is available for an additional fee and takes 2 business days.
International shipping is available to over 50 countries worldwide.
Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.
We are not responsible for delays caused by customs or weather conditions.

Payment Policy

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal.
All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.
Your credit card is charged at the time of purchase confirmation.
We do not store credit card information on our servers.
For large orders over 1000 dollars, we offer net 30 payment terms for businesses.

Customer Support Policy

Our customer support team is available Monday through Friday 9am to 5pm EST.

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days.
For urgent issues, please call our support line for immediate assistance.
Customer satisfaction is our top priority and we will do our best to resolve any issue.

Privacy Policy

We collect only the information necessary to process your orders and improve our service.
Your personal information is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
We use industry standard encryption to protect your data during transmission.
You can request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting support.
